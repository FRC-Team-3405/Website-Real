Welcome to FIRST, a community of students, parents, and teachers specifically driven by a passion for learning, scholarship opportunities, real-life career and business applications, and competitive sport! As a FIRST student, you will experience first-hand the impact STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) has on our community, society, and world. Join the next generation of leaders in the top global industries as they compete in an internationally-recognized and acclaimed Robotics Competition - this is FIRST, For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology.
